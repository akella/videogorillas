Careers

We suggest that you imagine working with us and not for us. We’re always looking for intelligent, creative and energetic achievers. You will be seriously passionate about media, hold a high-level degree in either computer science or maths, and know how to use that degree to solve real business issues. Alternatively, you may have taken an unorthdox route through formal education or be entirely self-taught and yet have a number of wow-accomplishments in video or audio programming under your belt. In either case, tell us more about it. In return we offer placement within a diverse team, a flexible and fun environment, yet one focused on a range of exciting, nontrivial projects. Of course, our remuneration packages are highly competitive and we offer multiple opportunities to develop.
